Subsidy Eligibility

  • Premium tax credits for income 100-400% of poverty level
  • Cost-sharing reductions for Silver plans
  • Medicaid expansion in participating states
  • Special enrollment periods for life changes

Open Enrollment Period

November 1 - January 15 annually. Missing this window means waiting until next year unless you qualify for special enrollment.
