Pros: Affordable, beginner-friendly, managed maintenance
Cons: Limited resources, slower speeds, less control

When to Upgrade from Shared Hosting

  • Site loads slower than 3 seconds consistently
  • Receiving 10,000+ monthly visitors
  • Need custom software installations
  • Experiencing frequent downtime
