Insurance Coverage Options

Like comparing different insurance plans, understanding your health coverage options is crucial for managing recovery costs effectively.

Medicare & Medicaid Coverage

  • Inpatient rehabilitation: 100 days covered per benefit period
  • Outpatient therapy: 80% after deductible
  • Home health services: Skilled nursing and therapy
  • Durable medical equipment: Wheelchairs, walkers, etc.

Faster Recovery Tips

Nutrition & Supplements

Proper nutrition accelerates healing. Focus on protein (1.2-1.6g per kg body weight), vitamin C, and zinc for tissue repair.

Physical Activity Guidelines

  • Follow surgeon's activity restrictions precisely
  • Start gentle movement as soon as cleared
  • Progress gradually with professional guidance
  • Monitor pain levels and adjust accordingly

Mental Health Support

Recovery can be emotionally challenging. Consider counseling services, which are often covered by health insurance plans.
