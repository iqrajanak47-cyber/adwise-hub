Fixed vs Variable Mortgage — Which Is Better?

Choosing between fixed and variable rates depends on your risk tolerance and market conditions. Similar to personal loan shopping, comparing multiple lenders is crucial for the best terms.

Mortgage TypeCurrent RateProsCons
30-Year Fixed6.85%Predictable payments, rate protectionHigher initial rate
15-Year Fixed6.25%Lower rate, faster payoffHigher monthly payment
5/1 ARM6.15%Lower initial rateRate uncertainty after 5 years

When to Choose Fixed Rate

  • Planning to stay in home 7+ years
  • Rates are historically low
  • Want payment predictability
  • Risk-averse borrower

When to Choose Variable Rate

  • Planning to move within 5-7 years
  • Expect rates to decline
  • Want lower initial payments
  • Can handle payment fluctuations
