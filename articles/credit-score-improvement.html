How to Improve Your Credit Score Fast

By Advise Hub • Jan 8, 2025

A higher credit score opens doors to better loan rates, credit cards, and financial opportunities.

Quick Credit Score Boosters

StrategyImpactTimeline
Pay down credit cardsHigh1-2 months
Remove errors from reportHigh30-60 days
Become authorized userMedium1-2 months
Pay bills on timeHigh3-6 months

Credit Utilization Rules

  • Keep utilization below 30% of credit limit
  • Aim for under 10% for excellent scores
  • Pay balances before statement date
  • Don't close old credit cards
