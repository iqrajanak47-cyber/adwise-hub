Small Business Loans Guide 2025

By Advise Hub • Dec 28, 2024

Small business loans provide the capital needed to start, grow, or maintain your business operations.

Types of Business Loans

Loan TypeAmountTermBest For
SBA 7(a)Up to $5M10-25 yearsGeneral business needs
Equipment FinancingUp to $5M2-10 yearsMachinery, vehicles
Business Line of Credit$10K-$500KRevolvingCash flow management

Qualification Requirements

  • Personal credit score 650+
  • Business revenue $100K+ annually
  • 2+ years in business
  • Strong business plan
  • Collateral (for secured loans)

Application Process

  1. Determine loan amount needed
  2. Gather financial documents
  3. Research lenders and rates
  4. Submit applications
  5. Review and accept terms
